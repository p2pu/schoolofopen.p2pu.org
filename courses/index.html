How can my course join the School of Open?

Do you have a course that should be part of the School of Open? Follow these simple steps.

  1. Check your course against the School of Open Guidelines. Still interested?
  2. Join the School of Open Google Group and send a draft of your course to the group and ask for feedback.
  3. Iterate and improve.
  4. Seek the final review of a School of Open organizer and make final changes.
  5. Share the link to the published course with the group.

We will add your course once it has undergone the review process. Read more about it here.
